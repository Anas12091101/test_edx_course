Fill the permanent license form and buy a driving ticket of rupees 100 from the post office and attach it with the form.

Also attach:

    1. 3 Passport size pictures
    2. Medical certificate
    3. A copy of your I.D Card
    4. Also attach 40 days learner permit as issued earlier

If you want to have a combine license for the motorcycle and car; it should be clear that the learner for car and motorcycle will be made separately and after 6 weeks the combined permanent license will be issued.
