Diver should be very careful before moving his car forward or backward or before opening his doors and had to make sure that there is no vehicle trying to overtake him. Also make sure there is no barrier or any child near your front and back wheels which are hardly visible.

The car drivers are supposed to use their mirrors before using indicators, before changing their track, before overtaking or before turning.

The bikers are supposed to take a look at back traffic even if they have side mirrors on their bikes before using indicators, before changing their track, before overtaking or before turning.
