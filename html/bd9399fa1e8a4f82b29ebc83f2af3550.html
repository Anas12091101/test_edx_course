For Motorcycles and cars

Fill the learner form and buy a driving ticket of 40 rupees from the post office and attach it with the form. Attach a copy of your I.D card. Age should not be less than 18 years. It should be clear that this permit will be valid for 6 months only. If you are well aware of traffic signs, indication and rules then you can apply for permanent license after 6 weeks. Applicants for LTV should be at least 21 years.
