A candidate should meet all these conditions.

He must know the meaning of all prevailing traffic signs. He must be familiar with all the traffic rules and signs. His listening ability must be good enough that he could hear all type of sounds and noises without the help of hearing aids and his eyes are able to differentiate between green and red color with or without glasses he should be able to read 7 alphabets or a number plate or 3 inches word’s size in a good day light from a distance of 25 yards.

Using his hands he must be able to indicate others about his intentions that what he want to do, or any driver who is not able to indicate with his hands must be able to use indicators on time for this purpose.

He should have understanding of traffic signals, and should be able to follow all the given instructions and take appropriate actions following signals given by other people on the road.

Condition 2 and 7 are not applicable on Motorcycle (Any vehicle without reverse gear). Candidate has to satisfy examiner that he’s aware of all the sections 85,86,87,88 and 89 of Motor Ordinance Issued 1965.

    1. He must know how to start vehicle engine.
    2. Must be able to drive straight or on a particular angle he faces.
    3. Must be able to overtake other vehicles, or can reach the vehicle moving ahead or can choose correct direction as an alternate track.
    4. Can turn right or left accurately.
    5. Normally can stop the vehicle easily. Or in case of emergency he should be able to stop the car on an appropriate place at the side of road.
    6. Can reverse the vehicle and doing so he should be able to take the car in a limited/narrow area at left or right side.
    7. He should be able to change the direction of the car in an opposite side by using forward or reverse gears
